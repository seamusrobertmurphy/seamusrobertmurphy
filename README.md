----------------------------------------------------------------------

I am a forester interested in enhanced forest inventories, carbon
modelling, landcover change mapping and spatial patterns of
disturbance ecology. Past repositories include resource & stakeholder
mapping, household consumption & nutrition, livelihood & value chain
analysis. Happy coding.

### Projects

-   [**VM0048**](https://seamusrobertmurphy.quarto.pub/vm0048/)
    **Jurisdictional REDD+** guide to Verraâ€™s consolidated
    methodology, including jurisdictional baseline emissions
    estimates, spatial deforestation risk mapping, and leakage belt
    delineation (*2025-01-04*).

-   [**VM0047**](https://rpubs.com/seamusmurphy/land-eligibility-hazard-degradation-check-VM0047)
    **ARR Feasibility** framework for assessing land degradation,
    forest change, and soil carbon stocks of potential ARR projects
    pursuant to VM0047 methodology (*2024-10-17*).

-   [**VM0010**](https://seamusrobertmurphy.github.io/VM0010-starter-template/)
    **IFM Template** for MRV reporting template for Improved Forest
    Management projects, with standardized procedures for inventory,
    carbon stock, and logging emissions reporting (*2024-11-04*).

-   [**VT0007**](https://seamusrobertmurphy.quarto.pub/vt0007/)
    **JREDD+ Risk Mapping** toolkit for mapping deforestation risk
    factors using satellite data and socioeconomic indicators for
    jurisdictional REDD+ programs (*2024-11-06*).

-   [**ART-TREES**](#0) **Demo Repository** showcasing best practices
    in version control for reproducible GHG calculation workflows
    under ART-TREES methodology (*2025-08-11*).

-   [**ART-TREES**](https://rpubs.com/seamusmurphy/art-trees-monte-carlo-uncertainty)
    **Monte Carlo** simulation tools for estimating uncertainty of
    REDD+ emission factors and activity data in compliance with
    ART-TREES Standard V2.0 (*2024-12-19*).

-   [**ART-TREES**](https://seamusrobertmurphy.quarto.pub/art-trees-allometry-validation)
    **Allometry Vallidator** for validating and calibrating allometric
    biomass models to comply with ART-TREES requirements, focusing on
    statistical bias correction (*2025-02-16*).

-   [**CFFDRS**](https://seamusrobertmurphy.quarto.pub/cffdrs/)
    **Canadian Forest Fire Danger Rating System** pipeline for
    reproducible wildfire risk mapping, fuel typing and burn
    prediction metrics in British Columbia's Okanagan Watershed using
    the updated CFFDRS V2.0 framework and `cffdrs` package
    (*2022-02-24*).

-   [**Map-Templates**](https://seamusrobertmurphy.quarto.pub/map-templates/)
    **Interactive and Static Mapping** of field operations, site
    visualization, and landscape analysis, including symbology and
    layout generation (*2024-12-04*).

-   [**LiDAR**](https://rpubs.com/seamusmurphy/gov_sk_tree_height_variability)
    **Forest Height** **Analysis** workflow combining LiDAR and
    photogrammetric data for forest inventory planning, including
    automated tree top detection and stand structure analysis
    (*2023-10-15*).

-   [**LiDAR**](https://seamusrobertmurphy.quarto.pub/lidar-forestry/)
    **Point Clouds Processing** workflow for High-Resolution Terrain
    and Canopy Modeling from Airborne LiDAR Data (*2021-11-09*).

-   [**Wetlands**](https://rpubs.com/seamusmurphy/mapping-endorheic-wetland-dynamics)
    **Delineation Using SAR & Landsat Workflow** for mapping endorheic
    wetland dynamics combining Sentinel-1 and Landsat time series with
    community ecological knowledge (*2024-06-15*).

-   [**GeoODK**](https://eu.kobotoolbox.org/#/forms/ajw7YvwXFgCgVaaAiz9Xrm/data/map)
    **Geolocated Survey Build** template using open-source mobile data
    collection tools for socioeconomic field surveys with customizable
    and automated quality control protocols (*2018-02-05*).
